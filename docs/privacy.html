Privacy Policy for Cats!

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Cats! ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Lady and the Cats.

1. What information does the Application obtain?

User Provided Information: The Application stores game data (such as high scores and "Best Times") locally on your device. If you choose to play online, this data is synced to a secure cloud database (Google Firebase) to preserve your progress.

Automatically Collected Information: The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application (e.g., game scores).

2. Does the Application collect precise real-time location?

No. This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

3. Third Parties

We may work with analytics and advertising partners (like Google AdMob) who need to know how you interact with the Application to display advertisements. We do not share your personal identity with these parties.

4. Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, please contact us.

5. Contact Me

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact me via email at writerrhea@gmail.com.
